## Why this group as a whole

The list deliberately mixes:
- **Original researchers** (Kevin Indig, Ryan Law, Chris Long, Mike King) — people generating new data, not recycling it
- **Working practitioners inside real companies** (Amanda Natividad at SparkToro, Garrett Sussman at iPullRank, Oleg at Stigan Media)
- **Consultants with real client case studies** (Aleyda Solís, Ross Simmonds, Lily Ray)

This avoids the trap of listing 10 people who all say the same thing — each person here represents a different vantage point on AI-powered SEO content production: research, in-house execution, community-building, and agency delivery.
